O-Cedar H2prO Flat Mop System

The O-Cedar H2prO Flat Mop System is designed to make everyday cleaning faster, easier, and more effective. Its dual-tank system keeps clean and dirty water separate, meaning every swipe uses fresh water. This feature helps reduce cross-contamination and ensures a deeper, cleaner finish. The high-capacity 1.2L water tank can cover up to 650 square feet with each full, making it ideal for large spaces and busy households.

The extra-wide mop head is engineered to clean more floor area in fewer passes, so you spend less time cleaning and more time enjoying your space. The included washable and reusable microfiber mop pad is durable, long-lasting, and easy to clean, replacing the need for disposable pads and saving you money over time. This is a great solution for eco-conscious and budget-savvy users.

Key Features
  • Dual-Tank System for Clean Water Separation
  • Compact and Lightweight Design for Easy Use
  • Built-In Water Jets for Powerful Cleaning
  • Sustainable and Battery-Free Operation
  • Washable and Reusable Microfiber Mop Pad
Why Choose This Mop?

Whether you're tackling hardwood, laminate, tile, or vinyl floors, the O-Cedar H2prO system is versatile and effective for all surface types. The telescoping handle allows for extended reach, giving you the ability to clean under furniture, tight corners, or high areas with minimal effort. Plus, the adjustable cleaning solution lets you customize your routine with your preferred floor cleaner or just plain water, making it a flexible and reliable cleaning tool.

Perfect For

Homeowners, renters, and families looking for an efficient, eco-friendly alternative to traditional mopping. This system eliminates the hassle of disposable pads, reduces water usage, and makes cleaning more convenient and sustainable. With its modern design and powerful performance, it's a must-have for any clean-loving home.
