Denqir 20V Cordless Electric Mop

The Denqir 20V cordless electric mop is a game-changing tool for effortless, deep cleaning of your home. With its 2-in-1 design, it combines a high-speed spin mop and a water spray system to handle dirt, grime, and stains on a variety of surfaces. The powerful water pump ensures even misting, providing a thorough clean without compromising efficiency. Plus, the built-in LED light helps you see clearly in dark corners, making it perfect for cleaning in low-light environments. The intelligent LCD screen gives you access to three power modes and real-time battery monitoring, so you can customize your cleaning session to suit your needs.

Effortless Cleaning for Hardwood and Tile Floors

This cordless mop features a self-propelled mechanism that makes sweeping and mopping large spaces more comfortable and less labor-intensive. The adjustable telescoping handle allows you to customize the cleaning height for optimal reach, while the 90-degree mop head gives you precise control without the need to bend or kneel. It is designed to be compatible with Dewalt 18V-21V batteries, providing you with an easy way to reuse your existing power source. With a robust 60-watt motor and high torque, the Denqir ensures you tackle even the toughest messes with minimal effort, keeping you comfortable during long cleaning sessions.
